Easy Guide to Use as a Warning App

Turn this site into an app-like experience for reliable alerts:

  1. Open in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS).
  2. Tap the menu (three dots or share icon) > "Add to Home Screen" or "Install."
  3. Allow location access when prompted for GPS alerts.
  4. Enable notifications and vibrations in your phone settings for rings/vibes.
  5. Launch from home screen and keep in background for real-time warnings.
